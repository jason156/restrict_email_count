Limit Out going Mails at Once

Useful to not to send all mails at once so that we can avoid burden on Mail Server and save Mails Servers from Hang-up.

Once mail got created and Sent by Odoo, immediately it won't be allowed to Send. It will fall in Out Going Mails Queue and Based on Configured Number in Emails Out going count in Company Configuration, only those many mails shall be picked and Sent.

But if you want to Send any Mail Immediately, then Click on Send Now button so that the Mail shall be Sent Immediately from Email Out going Queue.

Change Email Queue Manager Intervel Unit in Scheduled Actions to process Email Queue every Minute instead of every Hour.
